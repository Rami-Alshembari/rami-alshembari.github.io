Field Work Highlights

My field work focuses on active volcanoes, where I combine on site observations with high precision geodetic measurements. This includes GPS campaigns and deformation studies at Soufrière Hills Volcano in Montserrat and Sakurajima Volcano in Japan, as well as additional field campaigns focused on characterising ground deformation.
