Overview

My expertise lies in applying physics-based models to understand the dynamics of active volcanoes, aiming to significantly improve eruption forecasting capabilities. I integrate numerical modelling methods with geological and geophysical datasets to interpret volcano deformation during episodes of unrest and to reduce uncertainties in hazard assessment.

The DV3M project adopts a mush-dominated transcrustal magmatic system concept. My goal is to develop dynamic magma mush models that recover robust estimates of magma system parameters from observations, strengthening hazard mitigation for volcanoes such as Soufrière Hills (Montserrat) and Sakurajima (Japan).
