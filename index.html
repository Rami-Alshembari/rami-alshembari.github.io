Postdoctoral Research Fellow — DV3M University of Exeter Volcano Deformation Numerical Modelling Hazard Forecasting
Overview

I apply physics-based numerical models to interpret the dynamics of active volcanoes and reduce uncertainty in eruption forecasting.

Research Focus

Transcrustal magmatic systems, dynamic magma-mush rheology, and the interface between poroelastic reservoirs and deforming crust.

Approach

Fuse InSAR, GNSS, petrology, gas, and field observations with thermo-poroelastic simulations to decode unrest signals in near-real time.

Field Campaigns

Montserrat, Sakurajima, and interdisciplinary deployments across volcanic arcs to ground-truth model assumptions.
